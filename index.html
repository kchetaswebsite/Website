About Us

This website has been made to help children of today, to know about the history of the monuments and other structures. The website covers monuments from the Kakatiya dynasty to the Nizams, There are information and history of 8 monuments in the website, Charminar, Golconda fort, Qutub shahi tombs, Hussain Sagar, Mecca masjid, Falaknuma palace, Chowmahalla palace, Warangal Fort. There will be updated information of the monuments to keep our users updated on the monuments
